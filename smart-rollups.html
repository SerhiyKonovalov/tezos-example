Smart Rollups: Custom Layer 2 Solutions Secured by Tezos' Layer 1

Smart Rollups are custom-built Layer 2 solutions that leverage the security and decentralization of Tezos' Layer 1 blockchain. They allow developers to run any program as a rollup, making it fully secure and interoperable with the Tezos blockchain. This provides scalability, low fees, and a decentralized environment, while maintaining the integrity and security of the core Tezos network.

Why Smart Rollups?

Decentralization Taken Seriously

Smart Rollups ensure that decentralization is at the heart of your application. By utilizing Tezos' Layer 1 security, Smart Rollups allow your dApps or ecosystems to scale while preserving the distributed nature of the network. Power remains distributed across the network, with no central authority controlling the system.

Scalable, Secure, and Future-Proof

Tezos Smart Rollups offer scalability without compromising on security. They enable high-performance applications that can handle significant user growth and transaction volumes. Additionally, they are designed with future advancements in mind, ensuring that your applications remain relevant and operational long into the future.

Flexible and Developer-Friendly

Smart Rollups are designed to be highly customizable, offering flexibility for developers to create unique solutions that fit their specific needs. Whether it's a simple application or an entire decentralized ecosystem, Smart Rollups provide the tools necessary for building secure and efficient systems. Additionally, Tezos’ developer ecosystem is built to be accessible and user-friendly, streamlining the development process.

Unlocking New Possibilities for Tezos Developers

With Smart Rollups, developers can bring their most ambitious ideas to life. Whether you’re building decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms, supply chain solutions, or gaming applications, Smart Rollups offer a powerful and scalable foundation. They make it easier than ever to build on Tezos, ensuring that your projects are not only scalable and secure but also future-proof.
